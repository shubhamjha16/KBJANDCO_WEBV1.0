# How to Add Images to Your Law Firm Website

## Option 1: Download Free Stock Images

### Best Free Image Sources:

1. **Unsplash** (https://unsplash.com)
   - Search: "law", "justice", "court", "lawyer"
   - High quality, completely free
   - No attribution required

2. **Pexels** (https://www.pexels.com)
   - Search: "legal", "courtroom", "law books", "scales of justice"
   - Free for commercial use
   - No attribution required

3. **Pixabay** (https://pixabay.com)
   - Search: "law", "lawyer", "legal consultation"
   - Free, no attribution

### Recommended Images to Download:

## Option 2: Use Your Own Images

If you have your own photos:

1. **Resize images** to recommended sizes:
   - Hero images: 1920x800px
   - Team photos: 400x400px (square)
   - Logo: 200x80px

2. **Use online tools** (free):
   - https://www.resizepixel.com
   - https://www.iloveimg.com/resize-image
   - https://squoosh.app (best compression)

3. **Save with correct names** in the folders shown above

---

## Quick Steps:

1. Visit Unsplash or Pexels
2. Search for the image type you need
3. Download the image
4. Rename it according to the guide
5. Save it in the correct folder:
   ```
   my-website/
   └── public/
       └── images/
           ├── hero/
           │   ├── hero1.jpg
           │   ├── hero2.jpg
           │   ├── hero3.jpg
           │   └── hero4.jpg
           ├── team/
           │   ├── senior-partner.jpg
           │   ├── associate-partner.jpg
           │   ├── junior-advocate.jpg
           │   └── associate-counsel.jpg
           └── logo.png (optional)
   ```

6. The website will automatically display the images!
