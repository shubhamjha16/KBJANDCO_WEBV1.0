# Image Guide for KBJ & Co Website

## Where to Place Images

### 1. Hero Slider Images (4 images needed)
**Location:** `public/images/hero/`

Place these images:
- `hero1.jpg` - Supreme Court/High Court building exterior
- `hero2.jpg` - Law library or legal books
- `hero3.jpg` - Professional legal consultation scene
- `hero4.jpg` - Courtroom or justice scales

**Recommended size:** 1920x800px (16:9 ratio)

### 2. Team Member Photos (4 images needed)
**Location:** `public/images/team/`

Place these images:
- `senior-partner.jpg` - Senior Partner photo
- `associate-partner.jpg` - Associate Partner photo
- `junior-advocate.jpg` - Junior Advocate photo
- `associate-counsel.jpg` - Associate Counsel photo

**Recommended size:** 400x400px (square, will be displayed as circle)

### 3. Practice Area Icons/Images (6 images needed)
**Location:** `public/images/practice/`

Place these images:
- `supreme-court.jpg` - Supreme Court related image
- `high-court.jpg` - Delhi High Court related image
- `service-law.jpg` - Service & Employment law related image
- `labour-law.jpg` - Labour law related image
- `civil-criminal.jpg` - Civil & Criminal law related image
- `tribunals.jpg` - Tribunals related image

**Recommended size:** 600x400px (3:2 ratio)

### 4. Logo (optional)
**Location:** `public/images/`

- `logo.png` - Law firm logo (transparent background preferred)
**Recommended size:** 200x80px

## Image Recommendations

### Legal-Related Stock Photos Sources:
1. **Unsplash** (free): https://unsplash.com/s/photos/law
2. **Pexels** (free): https://www.pexels.com/search/legal/
3. **Pixabay** (free): https://pixabay.com/images/search/law/
4. **iStock** (paid): Professional legal images

### Suggested Keywords for Search:
- "Indian Supreme Court"
- "law books"
- "legal consultation"
- "courtroom India"
- "justice scales"
- "lawyer professional"
- "legal team"
- "Delhi High Court"

## After Adding Images

The website will automatically use these images. If the images are not found, placeholder colors/initials will be displayed instead.

To see changes immediately, the webpack dev server will hot-reload automatically!
